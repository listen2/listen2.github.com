Subreddit flair tracker

This tool automatically keeps track of userflair on certain subreddits and provides a mechanism to check whether a specified flair string is already in use. It is intended to assist moderators who wish to ensure that all userflair is unique. Potential collisions are ranked by Sørensen–Dice coefficient.

Moderators: Do you want more subreddits to be included? Do you want to see additional metrics? Let me know!
